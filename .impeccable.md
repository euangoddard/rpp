# Design Context

## Product

recipes++ — a personal/family recipe collection. Static Astro site, used at home
(often propped up in the kitchen mid-cook on a phone or tablet).

## Target audience

The owner's household and friends. Not a commercial product; no onboarding or
marketing surface. Users already know what they're looking for — speed of
finding and reading a recipe is everything.

## Use cases

- Search for a recipe by name or ingredient while planning or shopping
- Read a recipe while cooking (cook mode keeps the screen awake, hides chrome)
- Browse by category; star favourites; revisit recently viewed

## Brand personality / tone

**Warm editorial cookbook.** Like a well-set cookbook page: cream paper
background, refined serif display type (Fraunces), generous whitespace,
hairline rules, small-caps meta labels. The legacy blue accent (#226699)
is kept but warmed slightly. Friendly, unfussy copy. No cards-everywhere,
no glassmorphism, no dark mode.

## System notes

- Type: Fraunces Variable (display), Public Sans Variable (body/UI), both
  self-hosted via @fontsource
- Colors are OKLCH tokens in `src/styles/global.css` (`@theme`): paper, ink,
  accent, star amber
- Icons: inline SVG (no icon font)
- Tailwind v4 via `@tailwindcss/vite`
