## Ingredients

- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 celery sticks, chopped
- 6 large tomatoes, quartered and deseeded (or 2 cans tomatoes)
- 2 bay leaves
- 850ml chicken stock
- grated zest and juice of 1 orange
- salt, pepper and sugar (about 1 tbsp)

## Method

1. Sauté the onion and celery for 3-4 mins, until softened.
1. Add the tomatoes, bay leaves and stock.
1. Bring to the boil and simmer for 20 mins or until the vegetables are tender.
1. Remove the bay leaves. Purée.
1. Add the orange zest and juice. 
1. Season to taste (including sugar).
