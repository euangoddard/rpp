## Ingredients

- 1 large banana
- 2-3 large handfuls of your chosen fruit
- 1 pint glass ice

## Method

1. Crush the ice and put to one side
1. Liquidize all the fruit
1. Add the ice back into the blender and pour in the cream.
1. Mix one last time
