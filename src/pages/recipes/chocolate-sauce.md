## Ingredients

- 25g unsalted butter
- 2 tbsp cocoa powder
- 50g caster sugar
- 100ml double cream

## Method

1. 1. Melt the butter in a frying pan over a medium heat. Add the cocoa powder and caster sugar then mix together.
1. 2. Add the double cream and stir until smooth.
