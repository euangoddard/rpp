## Ingredients

- 100g flour
- 150ml milk
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp baking powder
- Handful sweetcorn (or other veg)

## Method

1. Mix all ingredients together in a bowl
1. Shallow fry a tablespoons worth of mix at a time
