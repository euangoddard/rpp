## Ingredients

- 43g butter
- 280g marshmallows
- 160g Rice Krispies cereal

## Method

1. Melt the butter. 
1. Add the marshmallows and  stir until melted. 
1. Add the Rice Krispies and stir until coated.
1. Press the mixture into a  13x9in. pan.
1. Cool until set.
